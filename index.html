About Me

Hello, my name is Jon Tousley. I was born in Rochester, New York in 1990. Shortly thereafter my family moved to Northern Virginia, where I was raised. Growing up, my passion was always soccer (I always dreamt of being a professional when I grew up), however, when I reached high-school, I gradually shifted over to focusing on playing guitar and music in general. After grade-school I attended James Madison University where I went on to graduate from in 2015 with a Bachelor of Arts degree. After college, I moved to Colorado and have been with a roofing company ever since. My job there is to collaborate with insurance companies to make sure the insurance provider returns the insured to pre-loss condition and pays a fair price. However, recently I have found a new love for computer science and programming and hope to make that my new career. Most of my free time is spent exercising, playing guitar, reading historical non-fiction, and watching soccer. My dream going forward is to have the kind of lifestyle that allows me to travel and see as much of the world as possible.
